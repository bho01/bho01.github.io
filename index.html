-About-


I am a high school student currently developing mobile applications (preferably android) in his free time. My programming journey sprung up in middle school, when a good friend of mine introduced the subject to me. I was interested immediately. I taught myself how to program with the help of books, websites, and other good programmers in my school. Before becoming an Android developer, I wanted to become web developer. So in my free time, I made small websites and projects that were primarily web based. But when I attended my first hackathon, MHacks, my team and I built an app that tracked finances. It was at that point I became interested in mobile app development. Currently I am a fresh Android Developer in my own time and a web developer at a gym called Newell Strength. The people at the gym are extremely friendly, the staff is awesome and the program is beneficial. I would highly recommend going there if you live in the area. Besides typing lines of code in my free time, I am a musician and an ultimate frisbee player.

My main goal in life is to help the community around me and the world that I live in. I strive to achieve that by improving the object that 70% of the world owns to this day: an internet-connected device.
